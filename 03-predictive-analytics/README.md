## Business Problem
Property review scores strongly influence booking decisions, yet hosts and platforms often lack insight into what drives these scores.

## Objective
Develop predictive models to estimate review scores and identify key drivers of customer satisfaction.

## Tools
- Python
- scikit-learn
- Ensemble learning techniques

## Models Used
- Decision Tree
- Random Forest
- Gradient Boosting
- Voting Ensemble

## Key Results
- Ensemble model delivered the most stable and accurate predictions
- Location and property characteristics strongly influence review scores
- Sentiment variables have limited standalone impact

## Business Impact
Supports pricing, property improvement, and service optimisation decisions.
