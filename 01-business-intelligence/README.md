## Business Problem
The organisation runs phishing awareness campaigns but lacks clear visibility into employee risk, training effectiveness, and response behaviour.

## Objective
Design role-based dashboards to support decision-making for HR managers, campaign managers, and employees.

## Tools
- Power BI
- Data modelling
- KPI design

## Key Insights
- Sales and Operations show highest risk scores
- Training effectiveness varies by department
- Reporting behaviour remains low despite high engagement

## Business Recommendations
- Target high-risk departments with advanced training
- Improve reporting culture
- Tailor phishing campaigns by location and role
