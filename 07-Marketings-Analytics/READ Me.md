## Business Problem
SmartStream needs to grow revenue and loyalty while reducing reliance on discount-driven promotions.

## Objective
Use SQL analytics to understand customer behaviour across loyalty levels, tenure, channels, and value segments.

## Tools
- SQL (CTEs, JOINs, window functions)
- Customer and transaction datasets

## Key Analyses
- Loyalty-level behaviour comparison
- Tenure-based lifecycle segmentation
- Channel preferences by gender
- RFM customer value segmentation

## Key Insights
- High-tier customers are least discount-driven and most engaged
- Bronze customers show strong upgrade potential
- Silver customers exhibit churn risk
- Engagement strongly correlates with spending and frequency

## Business Recommendations
- Personalised loyalty programs
- Channel-specific marketing sequences
- Reduced blanket discounting
