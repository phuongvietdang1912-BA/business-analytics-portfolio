## Business Problem
Managers need to understand which factors drive business performance and behaviour before making forecasting or optimisation decisions.

## Objective
Use descriptive and statistical analytics to explain historical patterns in sales, marketing effectiveness, customer behaviour, employee resignation risk, and production trends.

## Tools
- Excel
- Statistical analysis
- Data visualisation

## Key Analyses
- Sales drivers and cost relationships
- Advertising and promotion interaction effects
- Customer purchase behaviour patterns
- Employee resignation probability analysis
- Time-series trend and seasonality decomposition

## Key Insights
- Investment in staff and advertising strongly influences sales
- Promotions amplify advertising effectiveness
- Employee resignation risk decreases with experience
- Production follows stable seasonal patterns

## Business Value
Supports evidence-based decision-making by clearly explaining what happened and why.
